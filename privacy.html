MOS Mobile

Effective Date: January 1, 2025  |  Last Updated: June 1, 2025  |  Developer: MosTech

1. Introduction

MOS Mobile ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application MOS Mobile (the "App").

MOS Mobile is a companion app for the MOS (Motion Operation System) desktop platform, designed to control camera robots over a local network.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Camera Access

  • Purpose: The App accesses your device's camera for motion-sensing control features.
  • How: Camera data is processed on-device in real-time to detect motion gestures for controlling camera robots.
  • Storage: Camera data is NOT stored, recorded, or transmitted. All processing occurs locally on your device.

2.2 Motion Sensor Data

  • Purpose: The App uses your device's motion sensors (accelerometer, gyroscope) for motion-sensing control.
  • How: Sensor data is translated into camera robot control commands in real-time.
  • Storage: Motion data is NOT stored or recorded. It is transmitted only over your local network (LAN) to the MOS desktop software on your PC.

2.3 Local Network Communication

  • Purpose: The App communicates with the MOS desktop software on your PC over your local network (LAN).
  • How: Control commands and status data are exchanged between your device and the PC over the local network only.
  • Storage: No data is transmitted to external servers or the internet. All communication stays within your local network.

2.4 No Personal Data Collection

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Any data to external servers

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

4. Data Storage

All data generated by the App is processed in real-time and is not persistently stored. The App does not save any user data locally or remotely.

5. Network Usage

The App only communicates over your local network (LAN). It does NOT connect to the internet, external servers, or any cloud services.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The App is rated 4+ and does not contain any content inappropriate for children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

1. 引言

MOS Mobile（"我们"）致力于保护您的隐私。本隐私政策说明了您使用我们的移动应用 MOS Mobile（"本应用"）时，我们如何处理信息。

MOS Mobile 是 MOS（Motion Operation System）桌面端平台的伴侣应用，用于通过局域网控制摄影机器人。

2. 我们收集的信息

2.1 相机访问

  • 用途：本应用访问您设备的摄像头用于体感操控功能。
  • 方式：相机数据在您的设备上实时处理，用于检测控制摄影机器人的体感手势。
  • 存储：相机数据不会被存储、录制或传输。所有处理均在您的设备本地完成。

2.2 运动传感器数据

  • 用途：本应用使用您设备的运动传感器（加速度计、陀螺仪）进行体感操控。
  • 方式：传感器数据实时转换为摄影机器人的控制指令。
  • 存储：运动数据不会被存储或录制，仅通过局域网（LAN）传输至您 PC 上的 MOS 桌面软件。

2.3 局域网通信

  • 用途：本应用通过局域网（LAN）与您 PC 上的 MOS 桌面软件进行通信。
  • 方式：控制指令和状态数据仅在本地网络内于您的设备和 PC 之间交换。
  • 存储：不向外部服务器或互联网传输任何数据。所有通信均在您的局域网内完成。

2.4 不收集个人数据

我们不会收集、存储或传输：

  • 个人身份信息（姓名、邮箱、电话号码）
  • 位置数据
  • 设备标识符
  • 使用分析数据
  • 任何发送至外部服务器的数据

3. 第三方服务

本应用不集成任何第三方分析、广告或追踪服务。

4. 数据存储

本应用生成的所有数据均为实时处理，不做持久化存储。本应用不会在本地或远程保存任何用户数据。

5. 网络使用
