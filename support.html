MOS Mobile

Getting Started

  1. Launch the MOS desktop software on your PC and connect your camera robot
  2. Ensure your iPhone/iPad is on the same local network (Wi-Fi) as your PC
  3. Open MOS Mobile, then click the APP Control button on the PC-side MOS software
  4. Use the Control page for real-time motion-sensing operation, or the Keyframe page to program and replay camera moves

Requirements

  • PC running MOS desktop software (Windows)
  • Camera robot connected to the PC
  • iPhone/iPad and PC on the same local network (Wi-Fi)
  • iOS 15.0 or later
  • Camera and motion sensor permissions enabled

FAQ

Q: Why can't my device connect to the PC?

Make sure both your iPhone/iPad and PC are connected to the same Wi-Fi network. Some enterprise or guest networks block device-to-device communication. If the issue persists, try restarting the MOS desktop software and the app.

Q: The motion control feels laggy. What can I do?

Motion control relies on a stable, low-latency LAN connection. For best results, use a dedicated 5 GHz Wi-Fi network with minimal interference. Avoid crowded public networks.

Q: Does the app work without the PC software?

No. MOS Mobile is designed as a companion app for the MOS desktop platform. You need the MOS desktop software running on a PC with a connected camera robot.

Q: Does the app need an internet connection?

No. All communication happens over your local network (LAN). No internet connection is required.

常见问题

问：为什么设备无法连接 PC？

请确保您的 iPhone/iPad 和 PC 连接到同一个 Wi-Fi 网络。部分企业网络或访客网络会阻止设备间通信。如问题持续，请尝试重启 MOS 桌面软件和 App。

问：体感操控有延迟怎么办？

体感操控依赖稳定低延迟的局域网连接。建议使用专用 5 GHz Wi-Fi 网络，减少干扰。避免使用拥挤的公共网络。

问：没有 PC 软件也能用吗？

不能。MOS Mobile 是 MOS 桌面平台的伴侣应用，需要在 PC 上运行 MOS 桌面软件并连接摄影机器人。

问：需要联网吗？

不需要。所有通信均在局域网（LAN）内完成，无需互联网连接。
